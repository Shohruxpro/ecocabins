Innovation

We aim to continuously develop and innovate the EcoCabins, so that the EcoCabins are ultimately sustainable and circular. The condition for this is that the EcoCabins remain affordable with the help of the sun and the wind.

All EcoCabins have high insulation value, which is produced with sustainable and / or natural materials and have the 'round corners' as a striking external feature, which is made possible by the specific use of materials.
